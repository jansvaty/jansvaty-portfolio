Approach

Human-centred thinking, visual design, and code

I approach every project by understanding the people who will use it. Human-centred design thinking guides how I frame problems, explore solutions, and validate ideas with real users before committing to a direction.

That process sits alongside a practical technical toolkit. I move comfortably between interface design, content structure, and front-end implementation, which means fewer handoff gaps and faster iteration from idea to working product.

Growing up in Prague and building a career in New Zealand has given me a broad perspective on how culture and context shape design decisions.

I also actively pioneer, adopt, and experiment with AI tools to streamline and automate processes across my work. I document what I learn and share it with teams - from building AI agents in Copilot Studio to creating guidance and learning resources around responsible AI use - always alongside established best practices like usability heuristics and user testing.
